Submit Feed URL to GTFS Data Exchange

Use this form to submit an Open GTFS Schedule Feed for inclusion on GTFS Data Exchange. This helps developers find the URL where your data is published. The URL submitted will be checked daily and any feed updates will be listed on GTFS Data Exchange.

Note: It is also important to send an email to transit-developers@googlegroups.com with the URL of your feed to ensure developers are aware of it.

Note: if you do not have a website to host GTFS schedule data on, you may Upload GTFS Files directly to GTFS Data Exchange
